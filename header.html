Come see some
art.


If you like art and you’re around in Cape Town, come to our exhibition on the 23rd of September, 2022.

From 15:00 until 21:00, we will be displaying artworks by painter, Mlamli “Nytro” Ndlondlo all the way from eGqeberha. The series of paintings will be based on season one of the tribesoundradio mix; from “genesis” to “by any means.” Music for the day will be provided by Merchan & sethu.

There will be complimentary wines and snacks available. Baked edibles will be for sale. And entrance is free.

So come and feed your mind, body and soul at Co’Lab 22, the first official home of Isizwear. Tap here to add this event to your calender.

See you there...
