the future of radio.

Established in 2018, tribesoundradio was an idea that manifested into an online radio show two years later. Originally, it was just a 60-minute music mix that consisted of 'undiscovered' music, hosted by the founder, sethu. Now, tribesoundradio has plans of developing into an online audio-visual radio experience â€” redefining what radio is. While maintaining the concept of bringing you the best up-and-coming talent, we will bring you even more content, more shows, and more art. From South Africa to the world! We are here to stay.

Our Purpose, Our Vision &
Our Values

 tribesoundradio aims to unify the African diaspora of artists, thinkers, and consumers to encourage a movement of change-making for the better of our people, hence our tagline, 'the tribe is u.' Our vision is to be the best source of artistic talent, and intellectuals, on the rise. We always have our eyes out for 'the new', while showing the utmost respect to the old which paved the way for subsequent generations. To help us achieve our goals, we have a few core values which we continuously maintain:

  • Quality content - over quantity
  • Authenticity & Creativity - self-expression is key
  • Ownership - of one`s creative work and ideas
  • Curiosity & Wisdom - we encourage independent, forward thinking
  • Ubuntu, Peace & Harmony
  • Empowerment & Development - of Africans and Africa
